The 2026 International Conference on Advanced Technologies for Search
& Rescue and Emergency Response Systems (TSE 2026) provides a forum
for exchanging ideas, discussing research results, and presenting
cutting-edge innovations at the intersection of science, technology,
and emergency management. The conference brings together researchers,
engineers, practitioners, policymakers, and industry leaders from
around the world to address the increasingly complex challenges of
search and rescue operations and emergency response in the modern era.
The TSE 2026 will be held in Hanoi, Vietnam. Further information about
the conference, paper submission guidelines and templates will be
updated on www.tse.lqdtu.edu.vn.
## Paper submission

Prospective authors are invited to submit full-length manuscripts (4-6
pages), including figures, tables and references, to the official TSE
2026 website. All papers will be handled electronically. Accepted and
presented papers will be included in the conference proceedings with
ISBN. Papers are solicited in the following categories, but are not
limited to:

### Track 1: Artificial Intelligence, IoT, and Early Warning Systems
- AI/ML in forecasting, risk assessment, and real-time data analysis
- Sensor networks (environmental, structural, meteorological) and sustainable IoT in harsh conditions
- Intelligent early warning systems and multi-source data integration
- Emergency decision support and Digital Twin for urban areas and infrastructure

### Track 2: UAV Technology, Robotics, and Autonomous Systems for Search and Rescue
- Drones for surveillance and post-disaster mapping
- Robots operating in hazardous environments
- Human-machine coordination
- Practical applications and field testing

### Track 3: Command and Control Systems, Communications, and Cybersecurity
- Emergency communication networks (LTE/5G, satellite, etc.)
- Quantum Communication
- Emergency Operations Centers (EOC) and information assurance
- Cybersecurity for EOC, SCADA, and IoT
- Data security, system reliability, and best practice standards

### Track 4: Track 4: Search and Rescue Structures and Resilient Infrastructure
- Planning, design, and construction technology for search and rescue facilities
- Structures withstanding special loads and extreme conditions
- Technical infrastructure safety assessment
- Emergency energy systems and microgrids for sustained resilience

### Track 5: Simulation Technology, Training, and Digital Exercises
- Multi-level disaster scenario simulation
- VR/AR technology for emergency responder training
- Serious Games in Search and Rescue

### Track 6: Advanced Materials, Equipment, and Innovative Solutions
- New materials and personal protective equipment (PPE) for emergency forces
- Emerging technologies applied in search and rescue
- Innovative solutions from enterprises and startups
- Real-world deployment case studies and lessons learned

### Track 7: Advanced Technologies for CBRN Response and Environmental Security
- Numerical simulation, forecasting, and decision support systems
- Next-generation decontamination solutions and environmental recovery
- Environmental security and CBRN risk management
